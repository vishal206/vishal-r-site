- Watched Hamnet this week, and the closing scene – Agnes's gaze – was quietly moving. Beautiful in a way that stays with you. I think I'll keep my distance from the book, though; I'm not sure my heart can take more. A friend who'd read it noted the usual gaps that come with adaptations. If you're planning to experience both, start with the film.
