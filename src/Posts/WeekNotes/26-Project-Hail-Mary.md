Yo!

- A definite highlight of this week was watching Project Hail Mary. My first time in 4DX, which turned out to be a fantastic experience. I was hoping for a serious space movie, but the humor throughout made it an absolute joy; the entire theater was laughing together, which added to the fun. As someone who enjoys reading, the book had been on my list for a while. However, when the movie was announced, I deliberately chose not to read it beforehand, since reading the source material first tends to make people overly critical of the adaptation. Now that I've thoroughly enjoyed the movie, I'm excited to dive into the book and explore all the details the story has to offer.
