# Start Ember

Track your daily habits, see your streaks grow, and watch small embers compound into real progress, 1% at a time.

## Features

- **Daily Dashboard** — track habits with a visual progress bar, toggle completions, and see streaks at a glance
- **Calendar Analytics** — month-by-month view with completion rates, streaks, and retroactive date editing
- **Habit Management** — create, edit, and delete habits with custom names, icons, and colors
- **Authentication** — Google Sign In
