When I joined the company, I had an induction session which covered everything I had to know and also shared some documents. After a week, I had some questions, after a month, I had some questions, and even after one year, I had some questions. Mostly, I hit it on my teammates or track down an HR. And I saw the new joinees after me and my seniors too had questions. When a policy changed, people had questions. When a policy was removed, people had some questions. Most of the time, the whole team had no answer.

Interestingly, we all knew it was documented somewhere, and no one had time to find it.
Turns out, this is exactly the kind of problem AI is built for. Still, I couldn’t find a product like this that my company or any other could use. I took matters into my own hands.

I built a simple space where the HR uploads their documents and sends them to a Slack/Teams bot’s knowledge. And that space is Brudy.
